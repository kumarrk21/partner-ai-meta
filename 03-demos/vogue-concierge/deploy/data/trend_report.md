# Vogue Concierge — Spring/Summer 2026 Trend Report

## The New Power Dressing
The boardroom is getting a glamorous overhaul. Expect sharp tailoring softened with luxurious fabrics like velvet blazers, silk-lined trousers, and structured pieces in unexpected colors. The charcoal pinstripe is back but reinvented — think cropped vests layered over fluid blouses. Power dressing in 2026 is about confidence expressed through tactile richness, not rigid formality.

Key pieces: tailored vests, wide-leg wool trousers, velvet blazers, silk blouses with pearl details.

## Quiet Luxury Continues
The understated elegance movement shows no signs of slowing. Cashmere, fine merino, and full-grain leather remain cornerstones. Neutral palettes — heathered grey, caramel, slate, espresso — dominate. The emphasis is on impeccable quality and timeless silhouettes rather than logos or trends. A perfectly cut trench coat speaks louder than any statement piece.

Key pieces: cashmere sweaters, leather accessories, trench coats, minimalist sneakers, fine-gauge knits.

## Mediterranean Romance
Inspired by the sun-drenched coasts of Italy and Greece, this trend embraces flowing fabrics, warm earth tones, and effortless layering. Linen is the hero fabric — in jumpsuits, trousers, and wrap dresses. Terracotta, sand, and seafoam green define the palette. Oversized scarves and kimono jackets serve as versatile layering pieces.

Key pieces: linen jumpsuits, maxi skirts, wrap dresses, organza scarves, suede ankle boots.

## Modern Evening Glamour
Evening wear in 2026 takes inspiration from art deco geometry and contemporary sculpture. Metallic fabrics catch candlelight beautifully. Emerald green and champagne gold lead the color story. Accessories make the outfit — hand-beaded clutches, geometric statement earrings, and rose gold timepieces add the finishing touches.

Key pieces: cocktail dresses, metallic wrap dresses, beaded clutches, statement earrings, chronograph watches.

## The Denim Renaissance
Denim is elevated beyond the everyday. Wide-leg silhouettes in deep indigo washes feel both retro and thoroughly modern. The key is pairing premium denim with luxurious counterparts — a silk cami tucked into wide-leg jeans, or a cashmere sweater over structured denim. Dark washes and clean lines signal sophistication.

Key pieces: wide-leg indigo jeans, denim paired with silk and cashmere, clean dark washes.

## Color Forecast
The palette for Spring/Summer 2026 balances warmth with cool sophistication:
- Terracotta and warm earth tones for daytime
- Emerald green as the statement evening color
- Champagne gold for celebration dressing
- Seafoam green as the fresh neutral
- Lavender as the unexpected accent
- Cobalt blue for bold accessories
- Classic neutrals: ivory, slate grey, caramel, espresso

## Accessory Trends
Accessories in 2026 are investment pieces with personality:
- Structured totes in bold colors for the modern professional
- Chain-link jewelry in sterling silver — chunky and confident
- Tortoiseshell acetate sunglasses — the cat-eye returns
- Full-grain leather belts that develop character over time
- Rose gold watches with mother-of-pearl dials
- Pocket squares making a comeback in menswear

## Fabric Focus
The fabrics of the season tell a story of tactile luxury:
- Velvet for evening outerwear
- Cashmere and merino for elevated knitwear
- Linen for Mediterranean-inspired ease
- Silk and satin for luminous layering
- Chiffon for ethereal formal wear
- Duchess satin for structured cocktail silhouettes
- Organza for delicate accessories
